All Yarn takes care of all things yarn!

We raise alpacas on our little farm, take care of them and shear their wool. We manufacure the wool into yarn balls in enviromental friendly ways. We sell our yarn balls but also use them to make stylish sweaters which you can find in our shop.

We also have a little team of wood sculptors that craft our crocheting hooks and knitting needles.
